Help : Edit Request
How to assign or close this request
To assign this request, go back to the request detail page and click the Assign command. To close this request, go back to the request detail page and click the Close command.
Why am I not seeing all products
You can view all 'public' and 'private' products that you have purchased. Go to the Sales Manager to register a private product under your name. Once you have done so, this product should be visible in all product lists.
Different types of notes
Public notes are visible to all users. Internal notes are only visible to staff members and managers.
Co-submitter Email
A person who submitted a request may want all communications to be copied to other people. This works like the Cc: field of an email. You can enter multiple email addresses separated by commas.
Submitted To
The "Submitted To" field identifies the staff member who entered a request, received over the phone or by fax, for an End User.
