Root level messages are special
Root level messages may appear on the front page as news posted by the website operator. It is a good way to let your users know of important news, alerts and other critical information.
How to search by keyword
Type words or phrases into the "Keywords" text box, then click the Search button. Use commas to separate words or phrases. The system will attempt to find groups and messages that contain ALL keywords and phrases in their keyword, title or detail fields.
How to edit or delete a group
If you are a staff member or a manager, you should see the Group Detail command. Select this command to edit, move or delete a group.
How to move a group
Go to the group editing page, enter the new parent group ID, then click Save. If you are moving the group to the root, enter root, then click Save.
