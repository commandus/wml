How to search by keyword
Type words or phrases into the "Keywords" text box, then click the Search button. Use commas to separate words or phrases. The system will attempt to find groups and messages that contain ALL keywords and phrases in their keyword, title or detail fields.
How to edit or delete a category or an article
First, you will need to enter editing mode. Click the "enter editing mode" link in the upper right corner, then click the link of the category or article in the right pane of the window to edit it.
How to move a category or an article
Go to the category or article editing page, enter the new parent category ID, then click Save. If you are moving it to the root, enter root, then click Save.
How FAQ on the front page is generated
The FAQ is generated based on the number of visitors to each article. Answering 'yes' to the 'Did this help?' question also increments the visitor counter.
