Public and private products
Public products are visible to everyone. Private products are visible only to staff members, managers and end users who have purchased them.
For example, you might have a product called "MS Word". If John is an end user who owns a copy of MS Word, then there is a "MS Word" product registered under John in Product Registratrion. John will be able to see "MS Word" in all product lists.
Product dispatcher
A product dispatcher is responsible for dispatching requests to appropriate people to work on. Requests escalated by the Escalator will be sent to the corresponding dispatcher. New requests may be assigned to the dispatcher depending on the routing rules.
