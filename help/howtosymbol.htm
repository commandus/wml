How to insert a special symbol

You can insert a special symbol or symbol from different character sets, such as the trademark symbol (™).

To do this,

  1. On the Edit menu, click Insert symbol (or press Ctrl+Alt+Y short keys).
  2. Select one of the unicode blocks
  3. Click the character you want to insert, and then click Insert or just double click on selected symbol.

Also you can load keyboard layout in Windows, switch to Greek language, for instance and then type-in characters.
